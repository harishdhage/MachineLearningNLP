It is used to train Sentiment Analysis like spam email identification.

process : 
convert whole string to lower case -> Apply Stopwords -> Apply BOW to generate Vector

Advantages
1. We get fixed size (vector size) input
2. Simple and intutive

Dis-Advantage
1. Spars matrix or array lead to Overfitting
2. Ordering of the word is changed which lead change/lose the meaning of the sentence
3. Out of Vocabulary (New word is not considered)
4. symantic meaning is not captured (which word is important meaning in the sentence 
and which word need to come first)

To overcome cons, we can use below techinique
1. n-gram
2. Text-normalisation
3. TF-IDF
