Stemming is the process of converting a word to its root/base form. 
Other way it producing morphological variants of a root/base word.
 Stemming programs are commonly referred to as stemming algorithms or stemmers.
  A stemming algorithm reduces the words “chocolates”, “chocolatey”, and 
  “choco” to the root word, “chocolate” and “retrieval”, “retrieved”, “retrieves”
   reduce to the stem “retrieve”.

   Types
   1. PorterStemmer
   2. RegexStemmer
   3. SnowballStemmer
   4. LancasterStemmer
